KEY LOCATIONS IN AETHERMOOR

ASHENVEIL CITY
The capital of the Thornwood Kingdom, population approximately 200,000. Built at the confluence of the Ashen River and the Veil Forest. Key landmarks include: the Royal Palace (Thornwood Court), the Arcane Council Tower (tallest structure in the city), the Ember & Ash Tavern (Mira Coldwell's establishment in the merchant district), and the Ironclad Guild's Ashenveil chapter house. The city has been on edge due to food shortages and increased presence of strangers from the east.

THE EMBER & ASH TAVERN
Located in Ashenveil City's merchant district. Three floors: ground floor tavern and common room, second floor private dining rooms often used for discreet meetings, third floor residential rooms for long-term boarders. Known for excellent ale (the Ashen Stout) and Mira's famous lamb stew. A neutral meeting ground respected by all factions.

THE SHATTERLANDS
The fractured archipelago created by the Sundering. Hundreds of floating and coastal islands east of the mainland, many still magnetically charged with unstable magical energy. Dangerous to navigate. Home to strange creatures mutated by magical fallout, ruins of old Veilborn civilization, and reportedly, the hidden base of the Veilborn Remnant.

THE IRON REACHES
The northern mountain territories controlled by the Ironreach clans. Harsh climate, rich in iron and rare minerals. Not a unified state but a confederation of clans governed by the Iron Council. Currently suffering severe drought and eyeing Thornwood's southern grain fields with desperation.

STONEGATE CITY
Border city between the Thornwood Kingdom and the Iron Reaches. Location of the Ironclad Mercenary Guild's headquarters. A city of traders, mercenaries, and smugglers. Nominally under Thornwood jurisdiction but effectively governed by the Ironclad Guild's contract law.

THE VEIL FOREST
Ancient forest west of Ashenveil City that gives the Thornwood Kingdom its name. Believed to be suffused with residual Aetherial energy from before the Sundering. Strange lights are sometimes visible among the trees at night. The Arcane Council forbids unauthorized entry.
